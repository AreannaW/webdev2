About us

Our mission at Infused Berries is to bring the delicious, natural goodness of strawberries and the benefits of cannabis to people's lives through our range of premium edibles. We believe in creating products that are not only tasty but also healthy, sustainable, and enriched with the goodness of cannabis. We strive to source only the highest quality, locally grown strawberries and cannabis, and use them to craft our delectable treats. Our goal is to provide our customers with a guilt-free, plant-based snacking experience that is not only indulgent but also nourishing and relaxing. At Infused Berries, we are passionate about delivering innovative strawberry and cannabis-based products that delight our customers and contribute to their well-being, while also supporting local farmers and promoting environmental stewardship.
